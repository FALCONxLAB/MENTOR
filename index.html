Introduction

A Mission-Level Execution Architecture for LLM-Guided UAVs.

MENTOR enables UAVs to execute natural-language missions in real-world environments by connecting high-level semantic reasoning with reliable flight autonomy. The system compiles user commands into structured mission states and manages task progress through a guarded mission executive.

During execution, MENTOR integrates online visual evidence and skill feedback, then grounds validated stage-level decisions into typed UAV skills such as search, navigation, observation, localization, return, and recovery. This design allows large language models to reason over evolving mission contexts while mature autonomy modules handle flight-critical execution.

We implement MENTOR on a real UAV platform and evaluate it through multi-stage natural-language missions, demonstrating coherent mission context maintenance, heterogeneous skill coordination, and evidence-based stage transitions in real time.

让无人机从自然语言任务走向真实飞行执行。

MENTOR 面向真实无人机自主任务，将自然语言指令转化为结构化任务状态，并由任务中枢在飞行过程中持续管理任务推进、视觉证据和技能反馈。

系统把大语言模型的语义推理能力放在任务层级，底层搜索、导航、观察、定位、返航和恢复等飞行能力则由可靠的无人机技能模块完成。通过这种方式，MENTOR 能够在保持工程可部署性的同时支持更灵活的多阶段任务执行。

我们在真实无人机平台上验证了 MENTOR，展示了系统在多阶段自然语言任务中的上下文维护、技能协同和实时阶段转换能力。
